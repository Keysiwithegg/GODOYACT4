The Fast and The Furious : Tokyo Drift

The Fast and the Furious: Tokyo Drift (2006): Directed by Justin Lin, this is the third film in the franchise. It stars Lucas Black, Bow Wow, Sung Kang, and Nathalie Kelley. The story follows a high school student who moves to Tokyo and becomes involved in underground drift racing. This film introduces the character Han Lue, who becomes a recurring character in later films.
Cast: Lucas Black as Sean Boswell Bow Wow as Twinkie Sung Kang as Han Lue Nathalie Kelley as Neela Brian Tee as D.K. (Drift King) Sonny Chiba as Uncle Kamata
