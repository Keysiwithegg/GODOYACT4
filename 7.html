Fate of the Furious

Furious 7 (2015): Directed by James Wan, this is the seventh film in the franchise. It is notable for being Paul Walker's final film appearance following his tragic death in a car accident. The story follows Dominic Toretto and his crew as they face off against a vengeful terrorist seeking to exact revenge on them for their actions in the previous film. The film features spectacular action sequences and a touching tribute to Paul Walker.
Cast:Vin Diesel as Dominic Toretto Paul Walker as Brian O'Conner Dwayne Johnson as Luke Hobbs Michelle Rodriguez as Letty Ortiz Jordana Brewster as Mia Toretto Tyrese Gibson as Roman Pearce Ludacris as Tej Parker Jason Statham as Deckard Shaw
