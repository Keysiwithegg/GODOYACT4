Fast Five

Fast Five (2011): Directed by Justin Lin, this is the fifth film in the franchise. It stars Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Dwayne Johnson, and Jordana Brewster. The plot involves Dominic Toretto and his crew pulling off a series of heists while being pursued by a relentless federal agent. This film marks a turning point in the series, shifting towards more elaborate action sequences and a global scale.
Cast: Vin Diesel as Dominic Toretto Paul Walker as Brian O'Conner Dwayne Johnson as Luke Hobbs Jordana Brewster as Mia Toretto Tyrese Gibson as Roman Pearce Ludacris as Tej Parker Gal Gadot as Gisele Yashar Sung Kang as Han Lue
