Fast and Furious 6

Fast & Furious 6 (2013): Directed by Justin Lin, this is the sixth film in the franchise. It continues the story of Dominic Toretto and his crew as they team up with Hobbs to take down a skilled mercenary organization. The film features high-stakes action sequences and introduces new characters while bringing back familiar faces.
Cast: Vin Diesel as Dominic Toretto Paul Walker as Brian O'Conner Dwayne Johnson as Luke Hobbs Michelle Rodriguez as Letty Ortiz Jordana Brewster as Mia Toretto Tyrese Gibson as Roman Pearce Ludacris as Tej Parker Gal Gadot as Gisele Yasha
