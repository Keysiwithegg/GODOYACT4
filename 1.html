The Fast and The Furious

The Fast and the Furious (2001): Directed by Rob Cohen, this is the first film in the franchise. It stars Vin Diesel, Paul Walker, Michelle Rodriguez, and Jordana Brewster. The plot revolves around illegal street racing and heists in Los Angeles. It follows an undercover cop who infiltrates a group of street racers suspected of being involved in truck hijackings. This film introduces the characters Dominic Toretto (Vin Diesel) and Brian O'Conner (Paul Walker), who become central figures in the series.
